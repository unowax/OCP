# Soldiers Inc - Mobile Warfare

#### Do you want to get a high rank and become powerful in Soldiers Inc?

--------------------
**Level 1-8**

You're new to the game follow the tutorial and do what it says, do NOT engage or start fighting anyone. 

**Level 8-30**

Here's the first trick. As long as you are below level 30 you can log out and stay out of the game for 14 days, once you login again there will be a free pack which will last for 14 days with all kinds of gifts, including diamonds. This however requires that you do not login for 2 weeks. 

Get into an alliance and make sure you have rank as a private before you do this as your base will most likely get robbed. The alliance needs to be one which will protect you while you are absent. 

Do not engage in fighting any PvP with other players.

Stop worrying about rations, they will be negative,  but no troops will die.

Make friends and talk with the other members of your alliance. Say good morning be a nice person. Show the others you are here. Don't complain and be a problem for the management of the alliance.

Don't spend any medals or hero cash, nor intel files or building upgrade items. You will need them heavily from level 70+

Don't worry about rank, you are still just surviving.

**Level 30-70**

Nail every tournament possible by doing the least effort. Play all alliance and development tournaments and get the minimum required points do play Solo PvP and mecernary XP tournaments. Don't play Solo Ops or Solo Cache.

Piggyback on the top players to get the minimum required amount of points, this will get you best prize payout in tournaments. The tournament prize will fill you with resources and diamonds. Favor Razors and defense units for prize payout (you can convert them to offensive units later).

Research tech for defensive armor and aviation to at least level 12 (defensive power). Aim for getting Razor technology researched as this opens for tier 4 hero gear. 

If you are in an alliance with rocket targets then utilize this as much as possible. 

Get some mercenaries, only bother with orange and red ones. These are the important ones: 

* Marksman, Hitman (base defense/offense)
* Shield Android, Heavy Gunner (HQ defense/offense)
* Strategist, Pilot (Blacksite defense/offense)
* Saboteur (Recon defense/offense)

Get your Armor Depot and Hangar buildings to level 22. Get your Armor ATC and Combat Aviation ATC buildings to level 20. Spend your medals on your aviation units first and secondly your armor units in your ATC buildings.

If you have spare rations (from your inventory) then build Hellhound scouts, Sekiras and Firewolfs as often as you can and login.

See if you can make it to private as rank. Don't expect rank unless you make good relationship to the alliance management. You base is still only a potential for the alliance. 

**Level 70-90**

Prerequisite: 
* Razor aviation defense power at level 12. 
* Minimum 20 million power as aviation units to spare and throw away or into Special Ops.

You can now begin playing Ops seriously and get your tier 4 (gold) hero gear to max level. Three important things when playing Spec Ops. 

* Make sure your rations are max full before playing
* Only play Ops missions between level 70 and 135
* Only use aviation units

Stop playing when you get a good prize payout, unless you're on a mission to clear out everything :-)

You can begin fighting other players as you have the power to withstand attacks.

You should focus on developing your mercenaries and hero gear.

Get your Offense and Defense technology to level 20 along with your Attack and Defense Strength to level 10.

Only upgrade tier 4 hero gear with hero cash. Start using medals in your Combat Aviation ATC and Armor ATC buildings. Start with the units you have the most off.

Enhance your aviation in the blacksites you alliance has at it's disposal. Do NOT spend enhanced units in Spec Ops. 

Grow a farm or become part of one and make things easier for yourself. Get your Annexes to level 20.

Your rank is most likely between corporal and lieutenant.

**Level 90-100**

Prerequisite: Your hero gear is tier 4 and at max level.

You can now concentrate on getting your technology (Hiring speed) to level 20 and continue working on your mercenaries to get them to full power.

Your Wellheads and Munitions Factories should go to at least level 40.

Your rank is most likely captain or higher if you kept your relationships good and you have grown your base consistently since you began.

**Level 100+**

Get your Hangar and Armor Depot to level 25 and your Wellheads and Munition Factories to level 45. Continue developing your mercenaries and enhancing your aviation and armor units.

Your power levels should put you among the heavy hitters in the alliance.
